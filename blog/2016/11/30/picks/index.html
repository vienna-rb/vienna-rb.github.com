Picks / What the vienna.rb Team Thinks Is Worth Sharing This Week

11/30 Picks!

In a series on this website we’ll entertain YOU with our picks - or: what we think is worth sharing - every week. Books, articles, gems, fun stuff… you’re in for an eclectic mix! So, here’s for the one-hundred-eighty-seventh edition:

Markus
  • Let Postgres Do The Work - A demo of how you can offload work onto PostgreSQL, which can have the nice side effect of making everything much faster.
  • A Rubyist looks at Crystal (Part 2) - Continuing from Part 1, this post goes deeper into the unique features Crystal has in relation to Ruby.
  • Inspecting C’s qsort Through Animation - Various sorting algorithms animated, showing nicely how they work internally.
Max
  • That Works?! - Talk on quines, the true (ninja) art of programming (e.g. rewriting any JS program using only six chars).
  • Introduction to Concurrency in Ruby - Talk showcasing various options for having Ruby do things simultaneously.
